About

Built to Last, Inc. is a consumer technology company headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia. We design and build hardware and software for the modern connected home.

Our focus is simple: technology in the home should be calm, reliable, and genuinely easy to live with. We build products meant to be set up once and trusted for years — engineered for durability and a human-centered experience rather than novelty.

The company operates under the brand dead.simple. We're a small, independent team based in Atlanta, building deliberately and for the long term.

Our approach

Simple by design

Hardware and software that anyone can use without a manual. We remove steps until what's left just works.

Built to last

Durable engineering and long-term support — products designed to be lived with for years, not replaced each season.

Private by default

Your home is yours. We design for privacy and security from the first line of code, not as an afterthought.
